The story

Lifestyle sportswear retailer

Founded in 1974, Foot Locker Inc. is a multinational athletic footwear and apparel retailer with around 2,400 retail stores in North America, Australia, New Zealand, Europe and Asia. Foot Locker Middle East operates licensed stores in partnership with Alshaya Group and sells lifestyle apparel and footwear online.

The goal

Increasing omnichannel sales

Foot Locker Middle East wanted to increase both in-store and online sales.

The Solution

Omnichannel ads for two markets

To drive both in-store and online sales in two markets, Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates, Foot Locker Middle East decided to run a Meta Advantage+ sales campaign with omnichannel ads. Advantage+ sales campaigns are an advanced solution that uses AI and machine learning to automate, simplify and streamline the campaign creation, ad setup and account management processes.

Meta omnichannel ads are an optimisation solution that enables advertisers with existing omnichannel capabilities and a Conversions API for offline integration ‚Äì which provides the ability to safely send offline customer conversion information from the advertiser‚Äôs server to Meta‚Äôs server ‚Äì to optimise the objective of a single campaign for both online and in-store sales.

The omnichannel photo ads, some in the swipeable carousel format, showcased athletic footwear and clothing and highlighted price discounts. The ads also included a Shop now button that linked to the brand‚Äôs website, where people could browse products, add items to a cart and make a purchase.

Foot Locker Middle East showed the ads to a broad audience of adults aged 18 and over in Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates. The team also used the Advantage+ placements feature to automatically deliver ads across all of Meta‚Äôs placements based on which were most likely to drive the best campaign results at the lowest cost at any given time.

The results

Kicking sales into high gear

Foot Locker Middle East measured the results of its May 12‚ÄìJune 17, 2025 ad campaign using a Meta Conversion Lift test, which revealed:

  • 15.2X return on ad spend for omnichannel purchases (United Arab Emirates)

  • 6.3X return on ad spend for omnichannel purchases (Saudi Arabia)
  • 5.5X return on ad spend for in-store purchases (Saudi Arabia)
